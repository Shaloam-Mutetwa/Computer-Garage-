Computer Garage’s Privacy Policy

 

Computer Garage will, at all times, keep your Personal Information confidential and will only process information that is required to enable us to perform our obligations and provide you with the best possible service! We will only collect and use your personal information as outlined below.

For the purpose of these clauses, "Personal Information" shall be defined as detailed in the Protection of Personal Information Act No. 4 of 2013 (POPI).

By visiting www.Computer Garage.co.za (“Website / App”), you are agreeing to the terms of this Website Privacy Policy and the accompanying Terms and Conditions.

Computer Garage undertakes not to sell, share or trade any personal information collected online. Personal Information collected online will only be disclosed within our corporate group and service providers where required to perform a service to customers or improve your online experience.

It should be made clear that you have no legal obligation to provide us with any Personal Information and the submission of such information is entirely subject to your sole discretion and consent. However, if you will not provide us with the required information we may not be able to provide you with the information/services requested by you

If you sign up for Computer Garage’s newsletters or marketing mailers, you expressly consent to Computer Garage sending you marketing communications. You may unsubscribe to these marketing communications at any time by clicking on the link within the electronic marketing material or by contacting customer service or the Information Officer.

After placing an order Computer Garage will send you transactional emails for the purpose of tracking your order and keeping you informed. When placing an order you consent to Computer Garage contacting you for purposes relating to the order and performance of the purchase agreement. Transactional emails are not considered marketing communications and/or direct marketing.

Take note that Computer Garage sometimes partners with other companies and institutions (third parties) for promotions, campaigns and competitions. If you are subscribed to the third party database and consented to them sending you marketing material you may receive marketing material from the third party relating to Computer Garage even if you have unsubscribed to Computer Garage’s marketing material. Should a third party contact you with Computer Garage marketing material without your consent, kindly report this to Computer Garage (legal@Computer Garage.co.za) in order for us to investigate.

Any complaints regarding the processing of Personal Information may be sent to our Information Officer (legal@Computer Garage.co.za) and will be addressed as a matter of urgency.
